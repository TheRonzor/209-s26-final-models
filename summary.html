Class Summary

  • Tick marks for the means represent the 50th and 90th percentiles. Larger values indicate higher mean accuracy on the 15 hidden test sets.

  • Tick marks for the standard deviations represent the 10th and 50th percentiles. Smaller values indicate more consistent performance on the 15 hidden test sets.

  • Bubble sizes are inversely proportional to the gap between training and testing scores on the initial 2k5h set. Larger bubbles indicate models that would have been assessed as less overfit during training.

  • The best possible case would be a large bubble located in the lower-right corner (high accuracy, low variation, minimal overfitting on the provided training data). This is difficult to achieve with the basic MLP Classifier we were using!
